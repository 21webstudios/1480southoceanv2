The iCal link

What I need from you — and why it's safe.

An iCal link is the only thing required to get your availability calendar live. Here's exactly what it is and how to find it in about 60 seconds.

1

What is an iCal link?

A read-only feed of your booking calendar — the same availability information already publicly visible on your Airbnb or VRBO listing. It tells the outside world which dates are booked and which are open. Nothing else.

2

How to find it on Airbnb

Go to your listing → Calendar → Connect another calendar → Connect to another website → Copy the Airbnb calendar link → Text or email me the link.

3

How to find it on VRBO

Go to your listing → Calendar → Settings icon (top right, just above the word "Saturday") → Availability → Connect calendars → Export calendar → Copy URL → Text or email me the link.

4

What I do with it

My API reads the feed and updates your availability calendar on the site. It shows guests one thing: available or unavailable. No guest data, no booking details, no financial information passes through — ever.

🔒

Your data is safe

The iCal feed is one-way and read-only. My API cannot access guest names, contact details, booking amounts, or any private information. It reads exactly what any guest browsing your public Airbnb calendar already sees — open dates and booked dates. Nothing more.
